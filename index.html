Honko Damage Calculator

Damage Calculator for Pokemon cartridge battles, Gen 1-6.

If you are currently looking at Zarel/honko-damagecalc and not a fork, this is the official repository for the Pokemon Showdown damage calculator: http://pokemonshowdown.com/damagecalc/

Most notable for having all-vs-one (wall) and one-vs-all (sweeper) calcs.
